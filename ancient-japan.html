Ancient Japan

Ancient Japan refers to the period before the 8th century AD, characterized by the emergence of early Japanese civilization.

Key Features

  • Rise of early clans and tribal societies
  • Introduction of rice cultivation and pottery
  • Development of religious beliefs, including Shintoism
  • Influence of Chinese culture through trade and immigration

Major Events

  • Yayoi period (300 BC - 300 AD)
  • Kofun period (300 - 538 AD)
  • Introduction of Buddhism in the 6th century
